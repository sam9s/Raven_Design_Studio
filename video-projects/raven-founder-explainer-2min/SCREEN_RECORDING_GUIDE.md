# SCREEN RECORDING GUIDE — GREST IMS captures

What to capture, how long, and how to save. Four short clips totaling ~45 seconds — I'll use fragments of them (5–15s each) as visual proof while you narrate about GREST in the founder explainer.

## Recorder settings (one-time)

- **Resolution:** native 1920×1080 is perfect. If your IMS is at a different resolution (e.g. 2560×1440 on a larger monitor), record at that and I'll downscale.
- **Frame rate:** 30 fps (matches the video).
- **Output format:** MP4, H.264, reasonable bitrate (8–20 Mbps). Most recorders default to this.
- **Audio:** not needed — these are video-only captures. Disable the mic track in your recorder.
- **Mouse cursor:** show it. Natural movement is part of the proof.
- **Browser chrome:** keep it. The URL bar showing `wh.sam9scloud.in` is part of the story.

## Tools that work

- **Windows Game Bar** (`Win+G`) — free, built-in, good enough.
- **OBS Studio** — free, more control.
- **ShareX** — free, lightweight, lots of format options.

Any of these is fine.
